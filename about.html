About Wrecking Ball Games

Wrecking Ball Games is a small independent studio devoted to publishing small Android games on a regular basis.

Our games are for people who enjoy freedom. By that we mean people whose time is too valuable to look at ads or deal with the mental fatigue caused by predatory microtransactions.

We think those who appreciate the free, open-source software movement also fall under the umbrella of "people who enjoy freedom".

Wrecking Ball Games promises never to put ads in our games and to always release our games as free and open-source.
